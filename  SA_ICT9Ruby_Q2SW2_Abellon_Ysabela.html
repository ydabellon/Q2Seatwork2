Top 10 Dog Breeds that you should Adopt!

Samoyed
A samoyed is a cute and fluffy dog breed that loves to play all day with you. From running all day to cuddling with you all night.
Pembroke Welsh Corgi
A corgi is a short-legged dog breed with big ears and derpy mouth. In some countries, there are corgis with a fluffy tail or just a fluffy butt, also known as a bob-tail.
Shiba Inu
A Shiba Inu is commonly found in Japan, since it's the dog's breed origin. They are known for their fluffy wagging tails and sweet smiles.
Maltese
A Maltese is a cute small dog breed with a small tail and great jumping skills. But, beware, under that cuteness that dog can bark loud.
Doberman
A Doberman is a big dog with big bat-like ears and a small tail. People may find this dog breed teffrifying but under that posture is a little puppy at heart.
Pomeranian
A Pomeranian may look like a samoyed but the difference between them is that a pomeranian is smaller while samoyeds are bigger. They are also known as one of the fluffiest dogs and teddy bear-like appearances.
Dachshund
A dachshund (sausage dog) is also a short-legged dog just like a corgi. But, the difference between is that dachshund are much smaller than corgis.
Dorgi
Did you know that a corgi and dachshund have a mix? Well, they do! Introducing a Dorgi, a mixture of both dachshund and corgi, which they are famously known as the Queen's royal pets.
Border Collie
A Border collie is a large and intelligent dog full of energy. They are very outgoing and playful to families.
Dalmatian
A dalmatian is a big white dog breed surronded with black spots and are very known for being the chosen dog breed for the Disney movie "101 Dalmatians". They are energetic, smart, outgoing, and kinda loud.
